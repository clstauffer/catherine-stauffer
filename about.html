About Me

  1. Home
  2. About Me

I am a graduate research assistant and Ph.D. candidate under the advisement of Dr. Allison Wing. I obtained my Bachelor's of Science in Meteorology from FSU in May 2018 where I graduated cum laude, with Honors in the Major, and as a Garnet and Gold Scholar. My honors thesis, Air-Sea Interaction as Observed by Research Vessel Data, was under the advisement of Dr. Mark Bourassa. During my undergraduate career I was also a meteorological analyst for RMS H*WIND where my work included operational hurricane analysis during the 2016, 2017, and 2018 hurricane seasons. I completed a flight Master's of Science in Meteorology in December 2020.

I have lived in New Mexico, Colorado, Connecticut, and Florida and have close ties to Nebraska. I love the outdoors, especially any and all activities in mountains and rivers.
